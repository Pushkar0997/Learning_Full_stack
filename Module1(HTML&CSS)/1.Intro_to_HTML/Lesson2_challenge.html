Humans have reached mars!

The starship has successfully landed on mars.

The crew is safe and conducting experiments on the Martian surface. After years of planning and preparation, this historic mission marks a significant milestone in human space exploration. The data collected during this mission will pave the way for future manned missions to Mars and beyond. The crew is expected to return to Earth in six months, bringing with them invaluable knowledge and samples from the Martian surface. It took 115 days to reach Mars from Earth.
