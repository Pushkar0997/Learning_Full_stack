# Intro to Project

We will be making A Spiral Sounds Full Stack Express App. This is a music streaming service for classic vinyl that allows users to upload and share their music with others. Users can create playlists, follow other users, and discover new music. The app will have a user-friendly interface and will be built using ExpressJS, MongoDB, and React.
